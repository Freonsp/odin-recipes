How to Make Crack Pot Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2lbs. Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 oz. Packet Ranch Seasoning Mix
  • 16 oz. Cream Cheese
  • 8 Slices of Bacon
  • 1 1/2 Shredded cheaddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions

Instructions

Add the chicken thighs to the slow cooker, it should be 4 wuart or larger in size. Sprinkle over the ranch seasoning mix. Place the cream cheese on top of the chicken. Cover and cook on LOW for 7 hours without opening the lid during the cooking time. Shred the chicken with 2 forks right in the slow cooker. Stir the cream cheese and bacon into the shredded chicken. Spread out into an even layer. Sprinkle over the cheese. Cover for 10 more minutes to allow the cheese to melt. Serve over rice and sprinkle over the green onions. Enjoy!
